📖 How to Play OllieBoard
The Standard for Honest Work

OllieBoard is a meritocratic workplace game built on a simple premise: honest work, transparently measured, proportionally rewarded. Every mechanic on this board exists to make that real — nothing hidden, nothing arbitrary.

📞

Meet Ollie

Ollie is your AI voice agent. He calls the store's landline — no app, no hardware, no login required. When Ollie calls, he authenticates you by employee ID, delivers a task, and waits on hold while you complete it. A button press or voice confirmation closes the loop.

Why the phone? Every store already has one. Ollie meets the crew where they are — not where a software vendor wishes they were.
🎲

The Board

Each space on this board is an OA space — a category of tasks tied to Domino's Operations Assessment standards. Spaces unlock over time as your store proves mastery of each category. An unlocked space means Ollie is actively calling your crew on tasks in that category.

⭐
Stars
Each task completion earns points. Hit the star threshold and you earn a star on that space. Stars are permanent — they don't reset.
👑
Mayor
The teammate with the most completions on a space is its Mayor. Ties go to the longest-tenured team member. The Mayor's avatar appears on the board in Team View.
🐷

The Piggybank

Every completed task across the entire crew drops value into a shared Piggybank. No one is competing against anyone else. The crew builds value together — the way a Domino's store actually functions.

🏁

Pass Go — The Raise That Compounds

An employee who answered every Ollie call on their shift and completed every task type has gone all the way around the board. That's Pass Go.

Pass Go triggers a permanent +$0.05/hr raise. Not a bonus that disappears next pay period. A raise. It compounds every time you do it. This is the baseline stability in the system — reliable, predictable, earned.
✅
To Pass Go, you need to:
  • Answer every Ollie call during your shift
  • Complete at least one task in every unlocked space category
  • Confirm completion via button press or voice
🔄
Skipped Tasks
A task skipped three times arrives worth a minimum of 4× points on the fourth delivery. Opportunity accumulates — it doesn't disappear. There is no punishment, only deferred upside.
📋

The OA Connection

Domino's corporate conducts surprise Operations Assessments (OA) — scoring stores on brand standards. Your OA score becomes the multiplier applied to the Piggybank at cycle close.

This isn't an invented metric. It maps to something operators already care deeply about and are already rewarded for. That's the legitimacy anchor — the game runs on the same rails as the business.

Higher OA score = larger payout for the whole crew. When the team plays OllieBoard seriously, they're also naturally preparing for the OA visit. The incentives align, not compete.
🎯

Why Monopoly?

Monopoly is universally understood — across education levels, languages, and ages. It has the right emotional register: accumulation, board traversal, and collective building.

A teammate can immediately map their situation onto it: "I'm collecting points, building toward Pass Go, and the Piggybank is growing for the whole crew." That's intuitive in a way that "gainsharing tied to OA-weighted task completion metrics" is not.

⚖️

The Standard for Honest Work

OllieBoard exists because honest systems — where work is seen, measured transparently, and rewarded in proportion to contribution — create the most resilient organizations.

These game rules aren't just onboarding. They're a values document. They say: here is a system you can trust. Here is what you're building toward. Here is exactly how it works — nothing hidden.

That's The Standard for Honest Work made tactile.
